Askinosie Chocolate Packaging

Askinosie’s Chocolate Hazelnut Spread is so good that you should stop reading this and go buy some. Askinosie Chocolate’s branding is a bit industrial-looking like their factory and bold like their flavors. They take pride in having great rapport with their cocoa farmers, whom they feature on a lot of their packaging, like this jar.
