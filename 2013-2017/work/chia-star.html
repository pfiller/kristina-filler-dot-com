Chia Star

Chia Star is a superfood drink that is loaded with chia seeds. Chia seeds are nutritious and sustainable and have an incredible amount of other healthy attributes. The owner liked my Earth, Air, Wind, Fire Silkscreen, but wanted it to be punchy so that the label would really reflect the power of chia. So we combined that natural look with some bright colors, threw a few stars and lightening bolts in and vua la.

The Chia Star labels have been featured on The Dieline
