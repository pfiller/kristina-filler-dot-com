Scott’s Pizza Tours Branding

Scott’s Pizza Tours takes you on magical pizza-eating adventures throughout NYC. You can walk through historic neighborhoods or ride a big yellow school bus all the while learning about America’s pizza history, pizza facts and techniques as you taste the city’s most delicious pies. A tour this entertaining needed an engaging website to properly represent it. I’ve also designed Scott’s t-shirts and various printed materials.
